# AI/LLM Learning Path

> A structured guide to understanding modern large language models

---

## Week 1-2: Foundations

**Goal:** Understand what transformers are and why they work

1. Watch: 3Blue1Brown's "Attention in transformers" video
2. Read: [Attention Is All You Need](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762) (focus on sections 3.2, 3.3)
3. Concept focus: Self-attention, positional encoding, encoder-decoder

## Week 3-4: Modern Architectures

**Goal:** Understand how current models are built

1. Read: [DeepSeek-V3 Technical Report](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2412.19437)
   - Focus on: MoE architecture, MLA attention
2. Skim: [Gemma 3 Technical Report](https://storage.googleapis.com/deepmind-media/gemma/Gemma3Report.pdf)
   - Focus on: Local vs global attention, distillation

## Week 5-6: Reasoning & Training

**Goal:** Understand how models learn to reason

1. Read: [DeepSeek-R1 Paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2501.12948)
   - Key sections: R1-Zero (pure RL), cold-start data, GRPO algorithm
2. Understand: RLHF vs RLVR differences

## Week 7-8: Safety & Alignment

**Goal:** Understand how models are made safe and helpful

1. Read: [Constitutional AI Paper](https://www.anthropic.com/research/constitutional-ai-harmlessness-from-ai-feedback)
2. Browse: [Claude's Constitution](https://www.anthropic.com/constitution)
3. Optional: [Constitutional Classifiers](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2501.18837)
