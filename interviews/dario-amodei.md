## 2026-02-28 - CBS News Exclusive

After Pentagon blacklisted Anthropic:

On willingness to work with military:
> "We are still interested in working with them as long as it is in line with our red lines."

- No autonomous weapons systems
- 98-99% of military use cases are acceptable

> "We're gonna be fine. The impact of this designation is fairly small. Now, the nature of the tweet that the secretary put out was designed to create uncertainty, was designed to create fear, uncertainty, and doubt."

On being patriots:
> "We are patriotic Americans."
