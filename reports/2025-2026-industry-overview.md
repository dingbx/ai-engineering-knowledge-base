# AI/LLM Industry Overview (2025-2026)

> Market overview, major players, key research, and technical fundamentals

**Last Updated:** March 2026

---

## Executive Summary

2025-2026 marked a paradigm shift in AI: models stopped getting smarter only by training and started getting smarter by **thinking**. Key developments:

- **Reasoning scaling** via inference-time compute
- **Efficiency revolution** - frontier training costs dropped from $100M+ to ~$6M
- **Agentic AI** emergence as the dominant trend
- **Open-source acceleration** - gap with closed models narrowed to ~6 months

## 2026 Trends

1. **Agentic AI** - 40% of Global 2000 enterprises to adopt AI agents
2. **Reasoning over Parameters** - Inference-time "thinking" > bigger models
3. **Multimodality Standard** - All top models process text, image, video, audio
4. **Enterprise Adoption** - 75% of workers use generative AI
5. **Open vs Closed Gap** - Narrowed to ~6 months (was 1 year in 2024)
