# GPT-5 Rollout and User Backlash

**Date:** 2026-02
**Category:** Model Release

---

## Summary

OpenAI's GPT-5 launch triggered the largest user backlash in company history when they abruptly cut off access to GPT-4o and predecessor models.

---

## What Happened

1. GPT-5 family introduced into ChatGPT
2. Immediate cutoff of GPT-4o access
3. Users unable to access preferred models
4. Massive backlash on social media

---

## Sam Altman's Response

> "I think we definitely screwed some things up in the rollout."

Company learned lesson about abruptly cutting model access.

---

## GPT-5 Performance

Despite rollout issues, GPT-5 showed significant improvements:

| Model | vs Human Experts |
|-------|------------------|
| GPT-5 Thinking | 38.8% |
| GPT-5.2 Thinking | 70.9% |
| GPT-5.2 Pro | 74.1% |

GPT-5.2 Pro output preferred over human expert output nearly 3/4 of the time.

---

## User Attachment

Altman on X:
> "One thing you might be noticing is how much of an attachment some people have to specific AI models. It feels different and stronger than the kinds of attachment people have had to previous kinds of technology."
